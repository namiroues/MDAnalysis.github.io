MDAnalysis hosts and participates in various **community-driven events, mentoring programs, and workshops** to foster collaboration, learning, and contributions to open-source molecular dynamics analysis.

This page provides information on **upcoming and past events**, including **mentoring programs, technical workshops, and user group meetings (UGMs)**. Whether you're a developer, researcher, or new to the field, these events provide opportunities to **engage, learn, and contribute**.

For upcoming mentoring programs and deadlines, see the [Upcoming Events](#upcoming-events).

## Mentoring & Outreach Programs

MDAnalysis actively supports mentoring programs that allow students, researchers, and early-career contributors to engage with open-source development and scientific software.

### Current & Past Programs

- [Google Summer of Code (GSoC)](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) – A global, remote program where students contribute to open-source projects under mentorship.
- [Google Season of Docs](https://developers.google.com/season-of-docs) – Connects technical writers with open-source projects to improve documentation.
- [Outreachy](https://www.outreachy.org/) – A diversity-focused program providing paid remote internships.
- [Station1 Frontiers Fellowship](https://www.station1.org/sff) – A research and internship program for students in science and technology.
- [CompChemURG](https://www.bindingsites.co.uk/home) – A mentoring scheme focused on computational chemistry.

Many of our [core developers](/about/#mdanalysis-core-developers/) started through these programs—consider joining!
